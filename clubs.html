DECA is a not-for-profitstudent organization that hosts international competitions for over 200,000 students every year, testing students on their understanding of global finance and the economy, as well as their leadership and managerial skills.

FBLA Ages 14-18 Expensive

The Future Business Leaders of America is a national student organization that aims tp build the entrepenurs and leaders of tomorrow. With over 200,000 members and a concrete national structure, FBLA works to inspire and prepares students to become community-minded business leaders in a global society with their career and technical competitive events.

TSA Ages 14-18 Expensive

The Technology Student Association is a national student organization created to develop student's skills in science, technology, engineering, and mathematics as well as business education. TSA aims to develop leadership, academic, and business management skills in the workplace among students and leaders within the community. TSA hosts multiple regional, state, and national competitions for hundreds of thousands of students every year.

Model UN Ages 14-18 Expensive

Model United Nations, also known as Model UN or MUN, is an educational simulation and/or academic activity in which students can learn about diplomacy, international relations, and the United Nations.

Book Club Ages 14-18 Expensive

If you enjoy reading but never know what to read or simply want to challenge yourself and dive into more interesting or complex literature, this club is for you. Generally, a book club is structured so that a large group of people read the same book and meet regularly to discuss the book and further their understanding of the text, but these clubs can also be structured more loosely where individuals read their own books and simply give each other recommendations or talk about books they've already read,

Political Club Ages 14-18 Expensive

A political club is an organization where generally like-minded people can discuss and debate political issues and their government's stance on these issues. Political clubs generally consist of members from the same party but can have people with a diverse range of stances.

Comic Book Club Ages 14-18 Expensive

Interested in reading and discussing comic books and graphic novels with friends? Well a comic book club may be the right thing for you. Comic book clubs, generally located in places with access to comic book stores, are great ways to read comic books about your favorite characters with your friends, while also engaging in deep discussions and debates about these stories.

Foreign Language Club Ages 14-18 Expensive

If you are interested in learning a new language or experiencing the culture of a particular group of people from a foreign country, this is the club for you. If a popular language like French, Spanish, Japanese, or German is not taught at your school, consider joining a foreign language club where you can learn to speak and write in these languages and communicate with others who are learning along with you.

Photography Club Ages 14-18 Expensive

This is a club for individuals interested in learning about photography, the principles and techniques that professional photographers use, and the utilization of editing software to touch up these photos. Whether you've never picked up a camera before or you've breathtaking pictures of natural features, everyone can learn from each other's work at a Photography club. All you need is a camera and the interest to go out and explore!

Video Tech Club Ages 14-18 Expensive

This club is for those interested in producing their own video creations. In this club, members learn various techniques and tricks of filming, editing, and publishing videos for various purposes. Many Video Tech clubs even publish their videos online so people outside of the club can see their creations.

Film Club Ages 14-18 Expensive

Film clubs are common all around the world for people of all ages. Members of these clubs are individuals who not only have an interest in watching films, but analyzing them, discussing the techniques and work that went to creating them, and debating the proper rankings and comparison of different films.

Competitive Mathematics Club Ages 14-18 Expensive

If you are skilled in quickly solving complex math problems and memorizing tons of equations and theorems, this is the club for you. Of course, anyone can join a competitive math club but this club is generally meant for those who are really good at math and can learn and solve problems quickly in a stressful environment. There are many math competitions that students in these clubs can register for like AIME, AMC, and the National Math League, and many of these clubs like to have students form teams and train over a course of a couple of months.

Mathematics Club Ages 14-18 Expensive

Mathematics club members are people that have an interest for learning mathematics principles and theorems to improve their knowledge of the mathematical world. Math clubs can also be used as platforms for peers to gather and help each other out with their math homework, tests or other school work, generally led by a math teacher.

Ecology and Botany Club Ages 14-18 Expensive

This club is generally for those with a passion and interest to understand and learn more about the natural environment, specifically plants and animals. Members of an ecology and/or botany club generally partake in activities like planting seeds and crops, visiting public gardens or greenhouses, participating in discussions and debates about environmental problems, and many more engaging activites.

Physics Club Ages 14-18 Expensive

This is a club for those that are always interested in how everything in our universe works and interacts with each other. A physics club is a great way to learn more about the forces, energy, and objects that you interact with everyday, through hands on experiments and group discussions. Physics clubs may bring in professional physicists or physics professors and may even visit large scale physics experiments like observatories or particle colliders.

Biology and Anatomy Club Ages 14-18 Expensive

Members of a Biology and Anatomy club are students that are interested in the composition of living organisms, mainly animals like us humans. In this club, students will likely learn about the anatomic structure of humans and other living organisms and the physiological processes that drive these organisms. Some clubs may even take students on field trips to museums or hospitals, dissect organisms to learn more about their internal structure, or even inspect medical skeletons.
